About the app

Fence Sense helps fencers track bout results, review touch margin trends, and prepare for pools and tableau rounds — all on the day of competition.

  • Log bout wins and losses and see your win rate at a glance
  • Visualize touch margin trends across a competition
  • Organize pools and tableau brackets
  • Manage membership details

Frequently asked questions

Does Fence Sense sync my data across devices?

Bout and pool data is stored on your device. Reinstalling the app or switching devices without a backup will not carry your history over.

Which iOS versions are supported?

Fence Sense requires iOS 17 or later, and runs on both iPhone and iPad.
